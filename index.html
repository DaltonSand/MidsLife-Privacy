MidsLife – Privacy Policy

Effective date: August 12, 2025

Independence & Non-Affiliation: MidsLife is independently developed and is not affiliated with, sponsored by, or endorsed by the United States Naval Academy, the U.S. Navy, or the Department of Defense.

1. Scope

This policy explains how MidsLife collects, uses, and protects information when you use our mobile app and related services.

2. Information We Collect

  • Contact Information: Email address provided during sign-in (via Firebase Authentication).
  • Identifiers: User ID assigned by Firebase.
  • User Content: Workout logs, notes, and profile photos you choose to upload.
  • Usage Data: Anonymous usage metrics and crash reports (if Firebase Analytics or Crashlytics are enabled).

No Location Data: MidsLife does not request, collect, or store your location.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the app’s features.
  • To store and retrieve your data securely.
  • To troubleshoot issues and improve performance.
  • To communicate updates if necessary.

4. Data Storage & Retention

All data is stored securely using Google Firebase services. Your information is retained until you delete your account or request removal. You may request deletion by contacting us at the email below.

5. Sharing of Information

We do not sell or share your personal information with marketers. Data is processed and stored by trusted service providers such as Firebase, solely to operate and improve the app.

6. Security

We use secure connections (HTTPS) and Firebase security rules to help protect your information from unauthorized access.

7. Your Rights

You may request a copy of your data or its deletion at any time by contacting us at the email below.

8. Children’s Privacy

MidsLife is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from them.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updated policies will be posted with a new effective date.

10. Contact Us

Email: support@midslife.app
